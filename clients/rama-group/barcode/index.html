PIECC - Pune international exhibition and convention Centre.

- This project is amongst Asia’s biggest convention centres when completed. It is approximately 6 times the size of Pragati maidan.

- It will be constructed over 240 acres of land in PCMC’s newly developing residential and commercial hotspot Moshi.

- The objective of this project is to propel the industrial growth of pune and PCMC and bring international attention to the area.
